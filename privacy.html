Nova Cinematic — Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Nova Cinematic (“we,” “our,” “us”) provides an AI-powered service that helps creators generate cinematic videos and manage creative content on TikTok (“Service”). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account & OAuth: basic TikTok account identifiers (e.g., open_id) after you authorize via TikTok Login Kit.
  • Usage: logs and analytics about how you use the Service (e.g., feature usage, timestamps, error reports).
  • Content: videos and media you create or upload through the Service for the purpose of editing and posting to TikTok.
  • Contact: email or messages you send to support.

3. How We Use Data

  • Authenticate you with TikTok and operate the Content Posting API (e.g., upload drafts or publish when authorized).
  • Provide, maintain, and improve features (rendering quality, sync, reliability).
  • Protect the Service, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Communicate with you about updates and support.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers that help operate the Service (e.g., hosting/processing) under appropriate safeguards, or when required by law.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

  • You may revoke our TikTok access at any time from your TikTok account settings.
  • You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable.

7. International & Legal

Your data may be processed in the United States and other locations. If applicable laws (e.g., GDPR/CCPA) grant you additional rights, we will honor those rights as required.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance. Material updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
